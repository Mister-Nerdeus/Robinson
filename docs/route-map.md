- `/` - homepage with three conversion lanes.
- `/services` - service index.
- `/services/septic-cleaning` - septic request lane.
- `/services/well-septic-evaluations` - evaluation/realtor lane.
- `/services/well-septic-evaluations/realtors` - nested realtor support route.
- `/services/portable-toilets` - rental lane.
- `/services/commercial` - commercial services visibility route.
- `/realtors` - first-class realtor resources route.
- `/faq` - structured FAQ.
- `/contact` - general contact form route.
- `/admin/submissions` - local-gated review route.
